Our Story

The journey began in a small university campus where three students, Emma, Raj, and Lisa, were pursuing their degrees in Environmental Science and Computer Engineering. During a campus event, they observed the massive amounts of leftover food being discarded. This sight struck a chord with them, especially knowing that many people in their city struggled with food insecurity. Driven by the desire to make a difference, they brainstormed ideas to bridge the gap between food waste and hunger.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to foster a sustainable ecosystem where surplus food is efficiently redistributed, reducing waste and hunger simultaneously, while promoting environmental responsibility and community well-being.Minimize food waste through timely and safe redistribution.
